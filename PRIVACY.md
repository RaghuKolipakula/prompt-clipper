# Privacy Policy for Page to Prompt - AI Context Clipper

**Last Updated:** July 2026

"Page to Prompt - AI Context Clipper" respects your privacy and is committed to protecting it.

## Data Collection and Usage
- **Local Execution:** Page to Prompt operates entirely within your browser. 
- **No Data Transmission:** The extension reads webpage content solely when you click the extension icon to convert text into Markdown. No web page content, personal data, browsing history, or user activity is ever sent to external servers, stored remotely, or shared with third parties.
- **Local Storage:** Any user preferences (such as theme settings or formatting choices) are saved locally on your device via Chrome's local storage API.

## Data Sharing & Selling
We do not sell, trade, or transfer any user data to third parties.
